Water Content of Soil by Oven Drying Method

```

Aim

To determine natural water content of soil sample by oven drying method as per IS 2720 (Part 2).

Theory

Water content, also known as moisture content, is defined as the ratio of the mass of water present in a soil sample to the mass of dry soil solids, expressed as a percentage.

Apparatus

  1. Thermostatically controlled oven maintained at 110°C ± 5°C.
  2. Weighing balance.
  3. Desiccator.
  4. Airtight container.
  5. Tongs.

Procedure

  1. Weigh empty container (M₁).
  2. Weigh container with wet soil (M₂).
  3. Dry soil in oven.
  4. Cool in desiccator.
  5. Weigh container with dry soil (M₃).
  6. Calculate water content.
```
