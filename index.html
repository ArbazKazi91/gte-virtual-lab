Introduction

Welcome to the VCET Geotechnical Engineering Virtual Laboratory. This virtual lab provides an interactive platform for students, researchers, and engineering professionals to explore fundamental and advanced concepts of geotechnical engineering. Through virtual experiments, simulations, and learning modules, users can gain practical understanding of soil properties, foundation engineering, soil testing methods, and geotechnical analysis in a safe and accessible digital environment.

Course Objectives

  • To provide an interactive platform for understanding fundamental concepts of soil mechanics and geotechnical engineering.
  • To enable virtual performance and analysis of standard geotechnical laboratory tests and experiments.
  • To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications through simulations and hands-on learning experiences.

Course Outcomes

  • Understand the engineering properties and behavior of soils under different loading conditions.
  • Perform and interpret results of common geotechnical laboratory tests and analyses.
  • Apply geotechnical principles in the design and evaluation of foundations and earth structures.
